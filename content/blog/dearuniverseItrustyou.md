Dear Universe, I trust you. 

It's been so long since we've last talked and I don't even know where to begin. I'm about to graduate soon, and the uncertainty of the future is real. I told myself that I should never share feelings publicly, but for once I may should allow myself to do otherwise. I don't know where I'm heading, why I'm going to college, what do I want to study, do I want to pursue her...? I wish this blog post had more of a reminiscent value than it would serve as an outpour of my emotions though. I don't know what to say though. I feel like I'm slowly running out on my fuel. These lines don't even make sense.
